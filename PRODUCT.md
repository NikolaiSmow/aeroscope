# AeroScope

## Product Purpose
Real-time aircraft tracking and flight intelligence platform. Visualizes live aircraft positions on an interactive map with altitude-coded colors, route visualization, and detailed flight metadata.

## Users
Aviation enthusiasts, flight trackers, and developers exploring flight data. Technical users comfortable with aviation terminology (ICAO24, squawk codes, callsigns). Desktop-primary, used during active tracking sessions.

## Register
product

## Brand
- **Personality**: Precise, technical, authoritative. Think air traffic control meets modern data visualization.
- **Tone**: Confident and informative. No fluff.
- **Visual identity**: Dark operational interface. The map is the product; UI chrome stays out of the way.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS dashboards with rounded cards and pastel gradients
- Cluttered aviation tools from the 2000s
- Overly playful or gamified flight trackers (Flightradar24's consumer aesthetic)

## Strategic principles
- Map-first: every pixel of chrome competes with the map for attention
- Information density over whitespace: users want data, not decoration
- Glassmorphism is purposeful here (UI overlays map content), not decorative
